### Contextual Moat — 3/5
*Workflow depth × switching cost. Would users leave in a weekend if a competitor showed up?*

**Score rationale:**
Rationale: Deep integration with government travel workflows, approvals, policies, carriers, and financial systems.


**Named attacker (from partner challenge):**

### Data Advantage — 4/5
*Proprietary signal that compounds with usage. What do you see that OpenAI doesn't?*

**Score rationale:**
Rationale: Proprietary travel, pricing, compliance, satisfaction, and operational data, but the compounding loop is not yet fully activated.


**Named attacker (from partner challenge):**
Named attacker: A travel platform with stronger predictive analytics and automated recommendations.

---

### Platform Exposure —3/5

**Score rationale:**
Rationale: Core regulatory workflows and integrations are difficult to replicate, but standalone AI features can be commoditized.


**Named attacker (from partner challenge):**
Named attacker: OpenAI, Google, or a global travel platform offering AI-enabled booking and workflow automation.
